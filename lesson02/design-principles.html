Visual Hierarchy

Netflix

netflix.com

Visual hierarchy is the method of arranging graphic elements by order of importance. A good application of this principle makes the viewer's attention go where we want. On the Netflix website our attention is directed towards the center where the bigger elements are.

Fitt's Law

Google

google.com

Fitt's law consists in minimize interaction costs, that is, minimizing reading, scrolling, clicking and exploring to find relevant information. On Google we can see this principle well applied since the search bar is very easy to locate and we don't waste much time looking for it.

White Space and Clean Design

Chanel3

chanel.com

Using white space adds elegance to a web design, makes it more effective and makes your content ligible. Chanel's web site exemplifies this; you can see a lot of white spaces and this gives the feeling that the design is very clean and elegant.
